Writing Task 3#

Most of task 3 will be updating task 2 from the future to the past tense. As such, task 3 should be similiar in length and detail to task 2.

Differences are hightled in the task 3 template and discussed in the video. You must update it as follows:

Update title page, TOC, headers, and footers.

Rewrite corresponding sections from task 2 in the past tense adjusting as necessary following the task 3 rubric. All dates should be in the past.

Section B: Other Works must be rewritten using three works different from those used in section B of task 2.

For section F: Timeline, update the columns and dates from the task 2 timline table, and write a narative discussing how the timeline was met and any varainces.

Write section G: Unaticipated Requirements.

For section H: Conclusion, revise Outcomes from task 2 discussing the results and effect of the completed project.

For section I: Project Deliverables, add examples of deliverabels to the appendices.

Following APA guidelines, check grammar and sources, export as a pdf, and submit.

Submit, pass, and graduate!

Warning

Submission with missing sections are automatically returned. If everything went as planned, explain why. The a narrative following Timeline and an Unanticapted Requirments section are still required.

Section A: Overview#

Revise Task 2 section A3 adjusting for any variances and providing a summary of the completed project. You can reuse any content from realted sections C and D.

Tip

  • Write this section last or return to it after writing after the Conclusion.

  • The required depth of detail is up to subjectivity of the evaluator. Though summaries are typically short, there is no penalty for too much detail.
